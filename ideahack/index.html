Be The Smartest New Yorker.

As a group of the best and the brightest in New York, we aim to create a community atmosphere where like-minded people can come together and form lasting and meaningful connections, and have some fun!

Our non-profit organization's membership is open to people who score at the 98th percentile or higher on a standardized, supervised IQ, or other approved intelligence test. Over 200 tests are approved.

Our members include a professional baseball player, a competitive eater, a Jeopardy winner (okay, several), a Burning Man organizer, and an advocate of polyamory.
